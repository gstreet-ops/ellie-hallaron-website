About the Author

Ellie Hallaron is a romance author and lifelong storyteller, always turning ordinary moments into something worth retelling. She fell in love with books early on — the only punishment that ever stuck was having them taken away. Now she writes emotionally charged romance with depth, intensity, and heroes you probably shouldn’t fall for… but absolutely will.

Her debut series, The Syndicate, is a four-book dark mafia romance following four dangerous brothers. She writes for readers who want their romance dark, dangerous, and a little unhinged.
