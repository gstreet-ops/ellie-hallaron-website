About Ellie

The story behind the stories.

Ellie Hallaron is a romance author who specializes in writing love stories filled with complexity, intensity, and chemistry. Her female characters are always strong in their own way, and their male counterparts uplift them, even if they themselves are questionable at times. That's not to say their relationships are simple or not hard-fought for, but the battle always makes the ending more fulfilling.

While Ellie hasn't found her own happily ever after yet, she enjoys giving her characters the happy endings they deserve. Providing readers with finales they can celebrate brings her much joy. If Ellie can make even one reader swoon, sigh, or scream, then she's succeeded.

Ellie lives in the United States with her boxy puppy. Together, they wreak havoc around town. In her spare time, you can find her cuddling her dog, reading a plethora of romance novels, making a mess in the kitchen, or spending time with loved ones who patiently endure her spirals over fictional characters.

Ellie began writing after developing Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome. Running had been a passion, but when she was no longer able to do so, she began devouring books in her leisure. After a year of reading 125 books, she decided to give writing a whirl. She instantly fell in love with storytelling. Now, over a year later, Ellie not only has several books published, but now she's committed to a POTS training program that may allow her to run again.

Ellie enjoys sharing her journey with readers on TikTok. Posting clips from books, exclusive teasers, and day-in-the-life clips give readers sneak-peaks into her life. If you ever want to reach out to her, she'd love to hear from you via email or social media.
