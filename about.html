About Ellie

The story behind the stories.

Ellie Hallaron is a romance author and lifelong storyteller, always turning ordinary moments into something worth retelling. She fell in love with books early on — the only punishment that ever stuck was having them taken away. That love never faded. It just got darker.

She writes emotionally charged romance with depth, intensity, and heroes you probably shouldn’t fall for… but absolutely will. Her characters live in a world of power, loyalty, and moral gray areas — where love doesn’t ask for permission and the stakes are always personal.

Her debut series, The Syndicate, is a four-book dark mafia romance following four dangerous brothers bound by blood, ambition, and secrets that could tear everything apart. Each book follows a different brother as he meets the one person capable of breaking through every wall he’s built.

When she’s not writing, Ellie is probably reading, overthinking a playlist, or having a conversation with a character who doesn’t technically exist. She believes the best romances make you feel something real — that pull in your chest, that need to keep turning pages, that ache when the book ends.

She writes for the readers who want their romance dark, dangerous, and a little unhinged.
